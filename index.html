📜 Find Similarities between Documents 📜

This simple website allows you to find similarities between a document and a list of reference documents in order to detect plagiarism! 🔥

Usage:
  1. First convert your .pdf files in .txt with https://pdftotext.com/. (In future releases this step will be automated)
  2. Then Drag&Drop your files in the boxes below.
  3. Click on the Find Similarities button 💪
⚠️ NO FILES will be uploaded anywhere, all the computation will happen on your browser. 😊
